About Me

Caitlin started out going to culinary school with the intention of becoming a pastry chief. But after working in the industry for a few years she decided that she hated it and didn't want to take up smoking.

She decided to go back to school for graphic design. She graduated from Tunxis Community College with an associates in graphic design and she is currently in her "senior" year at the University of Connecticut where she hopes to graduate in the spring of 2026 with a bachelors in fine arts.

Afterwards she hopes to obtain a position at ESPN doing branding and creating on-air graphics for live events.
